More about my travels...

While studying abroad, I was able to visit London, Mykonos, Rome, Vienna, Athens, Delphi, Santorini, and Amsterdam.
Each destination offered something completely unique, which made every trip unforgettable. London was full of things to do, with beautiful parks,
famous landmarks, and incredible shopping. Mykonos had the most stunning beaches and sunsets I have ever seen, and the food there was amazing.
Rome felt like walking through history, with the Colosseum and ancient ruins around every corner. Vienna impressed me with its elegant architecture
and delicious pastries. Athens and Delphi gave me the opportunity to explore important archaeological sites and learn more about the origins of Western civilization.
Santorini was picture-perfect with its white buildings and blue domes overlooking the sea, and I loved every moment there. Amsterdam was charming with its canals, bikes everywhere, and fun city atmosphere.
Traveling to these places helped me become more independent, adaptable, and open-minded. I gained a deeper appreciation for different cultures and learned so much outside of the classroom.

  • My top 3 cities in order:
  • Mykonos
  • London
  • Paris
